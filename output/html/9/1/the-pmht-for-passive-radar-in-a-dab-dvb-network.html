The PMHT for Passive Radar in a DAB/DVB Network

Sora Choi, Peter Willett and Shengli Zhou

Read Article

Abstract

Passive radar using Digital Audio/Video Broadcast (DAB/DVB) signals with Orthogonal Frequency Division Multiplexing (OFDM) must contend with measurements of range and range-rate only (no, or very poor, angular information) and must deal with an added and unwonted measurement-illuminator-target association. But tracking systems using modified Joint Probabilistic Data As- sociation (JPDA) filters and using particle filters have been sug- gested and seem to work effectively to maintain tracks directly in the Cartesian domain. In this correspondence, we present an alternative Cartesian-domain tracking algorithm a version of the Probabilistic Multi-Hypothesis Tracker (PMHT), to contend with the extra-list data association in a natural way.
